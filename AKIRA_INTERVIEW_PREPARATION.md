Project Questions

Q. What is AKIRA?

A.
AKIRA is an offline AI voice assistant developed using Python that performs system automation, application launching, web navigation, and AI-powered question answering using a local LLM.

Q. Why did you build AKIRA?

A.
To automate computer operations through voice commands and integrate offline AI capabilities.

Q. What problem does AKIRA solve?

A.
It reduces manual interaction with the computer by allowing voice-based control and intelligent assistance.

Q. Why Python?

A.
Python provides extensive libraries for speech recognition, automation, AI integration, and rapid development.

Library Questions

Q. What is SpeechRecognition?

A.
A Python library that converts speech into text.

Q. Why pyttsx3 instead of gTTS?

A.
pyttsx3 works offline and provides real-time text-to-speech functionality.

Q. Why psutil?

A.
To monitor battery, CPU, and RAM usage.

Q. Why pyautogui?

A.
To capture screenshots and perform GUI automation.

Q. Why keyboard library?

A.
To simulate multimedia key presses for volume and media control.

AI Questions

Q. What is Ollama?

A.
A local AI runtime platform that runs large language models on a personal computer.

Q. Why Ollama instead of OpenAI API?

A.
Ollama is free, works offline, has no quota limits, and requires no API billing.

Q. What is Llama 3.2?

A.
A Large Language Model developed by Meta used for generating intelligent responses.

Q. What is a Large Language Model?

A.
An AI model trained on large amounts of text data to understand and generate human language.
